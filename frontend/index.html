A comprehensive forensic analysis of drug trafficking patterns across India from 2018 to 2024 — mapping geography, enforcement, and substance trends to uncover how narcotics flow through the nation.

📍

Why This Project?

India sits at the crossroads of the Golden Crescent and Golden Triangle — two of the world's largest drug-producing zones. Border and coastal states face disproportionate trafficking pressure, yet enforcement data is rarely visualized holistically. This dashboard bridges that gap.

🎯

What We Analyse

7 years · 36 states/UTs · 20+ drug types · enforcement KPIs · geographic risk scoring — all woven into an interactive intelligence interface built on NCB's official seizure records.

🔬

Our Methodology

Feature engineering on raw NCB data: Risk Index (weighted quantity + cases + diversity), Growth Rate (YoY %), Arrest Rate, Drug Diversity score, and Border/Coastal classification for geographic risk.
